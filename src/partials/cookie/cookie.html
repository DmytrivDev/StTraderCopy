What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand how visitors use our site, so we can improve and personalize your experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Site performance – to keep everything running smoothly.
  • Google Analytics – to understand how visitors interact with our content (no personal data is stored).
  • Marketing optimization – to help us show more relevant content and offers.
